Recognizing Common Mental Health Issues (The Guide)

Anxiety & Worry
What it looks like:

Frequent excessive worry, feeling restless, trouble concentrating, and physical symptoms like a rapid heartbeat or stomach discomfort. It's often future-focused fear.

More Info
Low Mood & Sadness
What it looks like:

Persistent sadness, loss of interest in activities once enjoyed, changes in appetite or sleep, and feeling overwhelmingly hopeless or tired most of the day.

More Info
Burnout & High Stress
What it looks like:

Feeling emotionally drained, cynical about work or life, reduced sense of personal accomplishment, and chronic fatigue that doesn't improve with rest.
